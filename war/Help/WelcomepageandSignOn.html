Welcome Page and Sign On
 Previous Top Next

The Welcome Page is the first page displayed in your browser when you navigate to the Autshumato TMS.

On this page, you can:

·    sign on;

·    enter the termbank; and

·    contact the system administrator or access the help file.


Sign On

To sign on to the Autshumato TMS click on the Sign On button at the top of the page (marked 1 in the picture above) and enter your username and password in the boxes that appear. Your username and password should be assigned to you by your system administrator.

Once signed on, you will have access to the following functionalities:

·    Change the source and target languages;

·    Browse through the Termbase;

·    Search for terms;

·    Filter the term records;

·    Create new records;

·    Edit existing records;

·    Delete existing records; and

·    Access the Administration Interface.


Each of these functionalities is described in its corresponding section in the rest of this manual.

Guest users may also enter the Autshumato TMS, but with limited functionality. Guest users can:

·    Search for terms (limited functionality)

·    Set the source and target languages.


Enter the termbank

Once you have signed on using your username and password or as a guest user, click on the Enter Termbank link (marked 2 in the picture above) to launch the Termbrowser and start browsing.


Contact the system administrator or access the help file

Should you have any difficulty in using the Autshumato TMS, you can either contact your system administrator or access the help file by clicking on the links marked 3 and 4, respectively, in the picture above.
